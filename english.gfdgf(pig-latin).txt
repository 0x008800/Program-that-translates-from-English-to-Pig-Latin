Onceay uponay aay imetay erethay ivedlay aay ionlay inay aay orestfay. Oneay ayday afteray aay eavyhay ealmay.  
Itay asway eepingslay underay aay eetray. Afteray aay ilewhay, erethay amecay aay ousemay anday itay artedstay otay ayplay onay ethay ionlay.  
Uddenlysay ethay ionlay otgay upay ithway angeray anday ookedlay orfay osethay owhay isturbedday itsay icenay eepslay.  
Enthay itay awsay aay allsmay ousemay andingstay emblingtray ithway earfay. Ethay ionlay umpedjay onay itay anday artedstay otay illkay itay.  
Ethay ousemay equestedray ethay ionlay otay orgivefay itay. Ethay ionlay eltfay itypay anday eftlay itay. Ethay ousemay anray awayay. 
Onay anotheray ayday, ethay ionlay asway aughtcay inay aay etnay ybay aay unterhay.

Erethay afteray, ethay ousemay anday ethay ionlay ecamebay iendsfray.
